GAME RULES
MINES

- A goal of the game is to reveal stars and avoid mines. With each revealed star, payout multiplier increases. Tiles can be revealed by selecting certain tile or clicking "pick randomly" button;


- Player can cash out winnings at any time by clicking "Cash out" button;


- Number of mines per field can be adjusted from dropdown. This affects game odds and bet multiplier;


- The maximum winning odds for this game are 5044291X. However, the maximum win is limited by the operator and can be accessed from the "Game Limits" section in the menu.

GAME INTERFACE


- Bets can be made from the bet panel, by selecting predefined bet amounts, or entering your own.Click(-) and (+) buttons to move between two options;

- Player balance shows available funds to play;

- Sound can be turned off and back on from the game menu;

- Bet history can be accessed from the game menu;

- Game information and rules can be accessed from the game menu;

- Provably Fair settings can be accessed from the game menu;

AUTO PLAY


Note: this feature might be unavailable in some casinos.

- Auto Play panel is activated by pressing the "Auto Play" button.

- In the Auto Play panel, the "Stop if cash decreases by" option stops Auto Play, if the balance is decreased by the Selected amount.

- In the Auto Play panel, the "Stop if single win exceeds" option stops Auto Play, if a single win exceeds the selected amount.

- After Start, remaining Number of rounds is displayed on the counter. The player can stop Auto Play at any time, by clicking the counter.

- Auto Play is activated by pressing the "Auto Game" button. To continue tiles on the board should be selected;

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION


- In the event of a malfunction of the gaming hardware/software, all effected game bets and payouts are rendered void and all effected bets are refunded.

- If the internet connection is interrupted when the bet is active, the game will auto cash out with the original bet amount or the current multiplier, and the winning amount will be added to your balance
