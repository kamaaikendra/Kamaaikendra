HOW TO PLAY
MINI ROULETTE

Just like classic roulette, but with better odds!

Select your chips, then place them where you want on the table and spin the wheel!

You can check the paytable for the odds.

GAME RULES
MINI ROULETTE

- Mini Roulette is a game of chance with a rotating wheel of 12 numbers, each with red or black background;


- Player can make a bet on a number, group of numbers, or different combinations;


- This is a single player game. When player made his bets, he can click the spin button to start the round.


- Player wins if one of his selected numbers or combinations matches the number hit by the ball;


- Win multipliers are displayed in the paytable.

GAME INTERFACE


- Player balance shows available funds to play;

- Sound can be turned off and back on from the game menu;

- Bet history can be accessed from the game menu;

- Game information and rules can be accessed from the game menu;

- Provably Fair settings can be accessed from the game menu;

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION


- In the event of a malfunction of the gaming hardware/software, all effected game bets and payouts are rendered void and all effected bets are refunded.

- If the internet connection is interrupted when the bet is active, the game will auto cash out with the original bet amount or the current multiplier, and the winning amount will be added to your balance

Note: Any Usage Of Illegal Tools to manipulate the game results may lead to account suspension.
