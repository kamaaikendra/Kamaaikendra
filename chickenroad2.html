How to play?
1.
Specify the amount of your bet.
2.
Choose a difficulty level in a game. The number of road lines and the chance to be hit car hard varies depending on the level of difficulty.

There are 4 difficulty levels in the game:

• Easy – there are 30 lines at this level.
• Medium – there are 25 lines at this level.
• Hard – there are 22 lines at this level.
• Hardcore – at the level of 18 lines.
3.
Press "Play" button.
4.
Your goal is to step as many road lines as possible without getting hit by a car. You can withdraw your winnings at any stage of the game.
