HOW TO PLAY
HILO

Guess whether your next card will be higher or lower than the last shown.

With each correct turn your winnings increase, and you can cash out after each step, or go for more!

GAME RULES
HILO

- The goal of the game is to predict whether the next card will be higher or lower than the current card displayed.


- If the next card drawn is exactly the same rank, it counts as a WIN for both "High or same" and "Low or same".


- Card rankings: 2 is the lowest, Ace (A) is the highest.


- After any winning prediction, you may Cash Out your current accumulated multiplier or continue guessing to multiply your winnings further.

GAME INTERFACE


- Bets can be made from the bet panel, by selecting predefined bet amounts, or entering your own;

- You can refresh your starting card before placing a bet.
